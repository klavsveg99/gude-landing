Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize your device and improve your experience on our website.

2. What cookies do we use?

We use the following types of cookies:

Type Purpose Duration
Necessary Enable basic functions, such as security and network management. Session
Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with the website. Up to 2 years
Marketing May be used for personalized advertising (in the future). Up to 1 year

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies in your browser. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View all cookies
  • Delete specific cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies

Please note that blocking all cookies may cause some website features to not work properly.

4. Third-party Cookies

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

5. Google Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to analyze website usage. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about how you use our website.

Google Analytics is configured in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy, and we use Google Consent Mode v2 to ensure GDPR compliance.

6. Google Consent Mode v2

We use Google Consent Mode v2, which allows us to adjust Google service behavior based on your consent. Cookie consent settings are stored on your device.

  • analytics_storage: Allows Google Analytics to collect data about website visits
  • ad_storage: Allows personalized advertising
  • ad_user_data: Allows personalizing data for advertising purposes
  • ad_personalization: Allows personalized advertising

By default, all values are set to "restricted" until you provide your consent.

7. Cookie Policy Updates

We may update this cookie policy in accordance with legal changes or our practices. The latest version will always be available on our website.
